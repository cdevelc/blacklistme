Email BlackList Service

What

Input your old email address and allow us to tell other legitimate email services that it is no longer valid. You are performing a service to others, helping them identify spam.

How

Use our home page to enter the address. We will send you an email with a confirmation URL. Click on that URL and we take it from there. If it is used in future email, it can be considered spam.

Cost

There is no charge for retiring an email address. This is a free service.
